About Me

About Me

With over four years as a pharmacy technician, I developed skills in time management, multitasking, and attention to detail—qualities that now shape my approach to design. Transitioning into graphic design wasn’t just a career change; it was a step toward my true passion for art and creativity.
My love for design has always been driven by the way colors, shapes, and ideas come together to tell a story. I strive to create designs that spark emotion, turn the ordinary into something extraordinary, and bring warmth and personality to every project. For me, design is about making connections—whether it’s with people, ideas, or moments—and I’m excited to continue this journey, crafting visuals that inspire and resonate.
