Paris Eiffel

The Eiffel Tower is an iconic landmark in Paris, France. Completed in 1889, it's a wrought-iron masterpiece and one of the world's most recognizable structures. Standing at 324 meters (1,063 feet), it offers breathtaking views of Paris from its observation decks. The Eiffel Tower is a symbol of Parisian elegance and attracts millions of visitors annually.

Paris Louvre

The Louvre is a famous museum in Paris, housed in the historic Louvre Palace. It's renowned for its vast art collection, spanning from ancient civilizations to the 19th century. Notable works include the "Mona Lisa" and "Liberty Leading the People." The museum features paintings, sculptures, decorative arts, and historical artifacts across various departments. With millions of annual visitors, the Louvre is a cultural landmark and a must-visit destination.
